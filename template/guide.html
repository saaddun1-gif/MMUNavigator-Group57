How to Use MMU Navigator

A simple guide for your campus journey.

1. Finding a Place

Use the search bar at the top of the screen. Type the name of a building, faculty, or room, and the map will show you exactly where it is.

2. The Main Menu

Click the menu icon (three lines) to open the primary sidebar. This gives you access to your user profile and navigation tools.

3. Using Categories

Click the Home icon to open the categories. You can filter the map to see only Facilities, Faculties, or Halls to find what you need faster.

4. Measuring Distance

Use the Distance Tool at the bottom right to see how many meters you need to walk. It helps you plan your walk between classes.
