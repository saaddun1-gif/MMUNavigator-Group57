About:

  • The "MMU Navigator - Your Smart Campus Map" acts like a digital map to help new students easily find places around the Multimedia University (MMU) Cyberjaya campus.
  • New students often feel lost because the campus is big and confusing. They waste time looking for classes, cafes, or the library.
  • The main users are new students at MMU Cyberjaya especially for WIN week.
