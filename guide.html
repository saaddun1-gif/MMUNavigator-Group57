How to Use MMU Navigator

A simple guide for your campus journey.

1. Finding a Place

Use the search bar at the top of the screen. Type the name of a building or a room, and the map will show you exactly where it is located on the Cyberjaya campus.

2. The Main Menu

Click the menu icon (three lines) to open the primary sidebar. This is where you can access your profile and navigation tools.

3. Using Categories

Click the Home icon to open the sub-sidebar categories. You can filter the map to show only Facilities, Faculties, or Halls to make your search faster.

4. Measuring Distance

Use the Distance Tool to see how many meters you need to walk to your destination. It helps you plan your walk between classes effectively.
